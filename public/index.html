Catch your lobster and eat it

Welcome to the ‘Catch your lobster and eat it’ virtual experience.

Using this tool, you have the opportunity to learn about the fishery in Ireland for European lobster, the biology and sustainability of lobster, and the culinary options available for making the most of caught lobster. The experience is very interactive and as you move through you will need to select various options, answer questions, and activate information points.

We hope you enjoy learning more about lobster!
